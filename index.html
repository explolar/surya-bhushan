§01 — Professional Profile

A career in solution.

Twenty years of hands-on and managerial work across the recombinant therapeutics industry — process development and technology transfer of biosimilars and innovator molecules spanning macular degeneration, oncology, diabetes and osteoporosis. From hyaluronidase to ranibizumab; insulin glargine to nivolumab; gram-scale benchwork to two-thousand-litre commercial production.

Deep strengths in process and impurity characterisation, bio-similarity to meet EMA and US-FDA requirements, in-process method development, pilot-scale transfer, optimisation, and the authorship of the documents that carry a molecule through regulatory review.

Collaboration, planning, presentation and troubleshooting define the working method. The instruments — chromatography, refolding, TFF, lyophilisation — are familiar from millilitre to multi-thousand-litre scale. Currently leading a team of twenty-five across twelve concurrent projects at Intas Pharmaceuticals.
